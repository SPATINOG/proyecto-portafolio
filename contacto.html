ABOUT

Sebastian Patiño is a visual designer and photographer based in Mexico City. Working locally and internationally, offering creative direction, photography and graphic design. I engage in ongoing partnerships and in creating a network of creative talent by working in a collaborative way. Recent commissions include creative direction, photography for Centro, visual identity for the LesGaiCineMad Festival.
